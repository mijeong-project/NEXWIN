Korea’s First QD+LD+Mini LED Smart Electronic Blackboard Launched

*Expected to launch in the first half of 2025

  • 💡 What is a QLED Electronic Blackboard?

    Unlike existing DLED products that use standard LED backlights, QLED enhances image quality by adding nanoparticles called quantum dots (nanometer-sized particles).

  • 💡 What Makes QLED Different?

    QLED actively utilizes the wide color gamut advantage of quantum dots. It improves brightness and color expression beyond conventional limits, delivering brighter and more vivid images.
    You can even see fine details clearly in dark areas of the screen.

  • 💡 What’s Special About Mini LED?

    Mini LED combines the strengths of LCD and OLED.
    Unlike traditional QLEDs, it uses LED backlights that are 1/6 the size of standard LEDs, providing superior brightness, excellent contrast, and outstanding color expression. QD+Mini LED is an advanced next-generation display technology that surpasses the limitations of existing backlight methods.
